Privacy Policy

Last updated · March 2026

Arrivo is a proximity-based arrival alert application designed to notify users when they are approaching a selected destination. The app helps commuters, travelers, and public transport users avoid missing their stops by triggering alerts when they are near their chosen location.

Your privacy is extremely important. Arrivo follows a privacy-first design where personal information is not collected, accounts are not required, and most processing occurs directly on your device.

This page explains how Arrivo uses permissions, services, and limited third-party integrations to deliver its functionality.

📍 Location Usage

Arrivo uses your device location to determine when you are approaching your selected destination and to trigger arrival alerts.

  • Detect proximity to the selected destination
  • Calculate distance during an active tracking session
  • Trigger arrival alerts at the configured radius

Location information is processed in real time on the device and is not stored on Arrivo servers.

🌙 Background Location

During an active tracking session, Arrivo may access location in the background so that arrival alerts continue to work even when:

  • The device screen is locked
  • The app is minimized
  • Another application is being used

Background location is used only while tracking is active and stops immediately when tracking ends.

🔔 Notifications

Arrivo uses notifications to communicate important tracking events including:

  • Active tracking status
  • Live distance updates
  • Arrival alerts
  • Missed destination warnings

Full-screen alerts may be used for arrival notifications to ensure the user notices when their destination is near.

🔎 Search & Maps Integration

Arrivo allows users to search for destinations using Google Places services.

  • Search queries are sent directly to Google Places API
  • Arrivo does not store search queries on its own servers
  • Locations shared from Google Maps may be used as destinations
📢 Advertising

Arrivo may display advertisements using Google AdMob to support development and maintenance of the application.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as device identifiers or advertising ID to deliver advertisements.

More information about Google's advertising policies can be found at:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

💾 Local Storage

Arrivo stores limited information locally on your device in order to function properly.

  • Recently searched destinations
  • Selected alert radius
  • User preferences
  • Temporary tracking session data

This information is stored securely using Android local storage technologies such as Room Database and DataStore.

🔥 Firebase Services
Analytics
Used to understand anonymous usage patterns and improve app features.
Crashlytics
Used to collect crash reports and technical diagnostics to improve stability.
Remote Config
Allows configuration values such as search quotas and advertisement settings to be updated without requiring a full app update.
AdMob
Used to display advertisements within the application.
⚡ Foreground Service & Live Notifications

Arrivo uses a foreground service during active tracking sessions to maintain reliable location updates and arrival alerts.

This service displays a persistent notification showing tracking status and ensures the system does not stop tracking while you are travelling.

🛡️ Your Control
  • You can stop tracking at any time
  • You can revoke permissions from device settings
  • You can control notification preferences

If location permissions are disabled, arrival alerts will not function until permission is restored.
