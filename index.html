Arrivo – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Arrivo. Arrivo is a proximity-based arrival alert application for Android that notifies you when you are approaching a selected destination. This Privacy Policy explains how Arrivo handles information in connection with your use of the app.

Summary: Arrivo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers. All data remains on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Arrivo does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Account credentials (no user accounts are required)
  • Device identifiers sent to our servers
  • Location history or GPS traces uploaded to our servers
  • Contacts, photos, media, or files
  • Browsing history or usage analytics (beyond what third-party SDKs may collect — see sections below)

Location Data Usage

Arrivo requires access to your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) to calculate the distance between your current position and your chosen destination. Location data is used solely on-device to determine proximity and trigger arrival alerts.

Your location is never uploaded to our servers. Location data is processed in real time on your device and is not stored beyond the current active tracking session.

Background Location Usage

Arrivo requests background location access (ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION) to continue monitoring your proximity to the selected destination while the app is not in the foreground. This allows the app to alert you even when you switch to other apps or lock your screen.

Background location is only active while a tracking session is explicitly started by you. Once tracking is stopped or completed, background location access is no longer used. Arrivo runs a foreground service with a persistent notification during active tracking so you are always aware when location is being used.

Notifications

Arrivo uses device notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) to:

  • Display a persistent foreground service notification during active tracking sessions.
  • Deliver an arrival alert notification when you approach your destination.

You can manage notification permissions through your device's system settings. Disabling notifications may prevent arrival alerts from being delivered.

Google Places API Usage

Arrivo uses the Google Places API to provide destination search functionality. When you search for a destination, your search query is sent to Google's servers to return relevant place suggestions. This interaction is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Arrivo does not store or log your search queries on any server operated by us.

Route Calculation (OSRM)

To estimate the route distance between your current location and your destination, Arrivo uses the OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine) routing API. Your current coordinates and destination coordinates may be sent to the OSRM service to calculate a route.

OSRM is an open-source routing engine. Arrivo does not store routing requests or responses on any server operated by us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Arrivo displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • General location (coarse, not precise)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App interaction data

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google's advertising policies. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings under Settings > Google > Ads.

Firebase Remote Config

Arrivo uses Firebase Remote Config to remotely manage app configuration values (such as feature flags and thresholds) without requiring an app update. Firebase Remote Config may collect minimal device and app information to serve the correct configuration. This is subject to Google Firebase's Privacy Policy.

No personal information is associated with Remote Config requests.

Local Data Storage

All user preferences and app state (such as the last searched destination or alert threshold settings) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device through Arrivo's systems.

Uninstalling Arrivo will remove all locally stored app data from your device.

Permissions Used

Arrivo requests the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION – To determine your precise location during tracking.
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION – Fallback location access.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION – To continue tracking in the background while a session is active.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – To run a foreground service during active tracking.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION – Required for foreground services that access location.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To display tracking status and arrival alert notifications.
  • INTERNET – Required for Google Places search, OSRM routing, Firebase Remote Config, and AdMob.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – To handle any necessary initialization after device restart.

Children's Privacy

Arrivo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since Arrivo does not collect or transmit personal data to our own servers, there is no personal data stored by us to secure. All data that remains on your device is protected by Android's built-in security mechanisms. For data handled by third-party services (Google Places, OSRM, AdMob, Firebase), their respective security practices apply.
